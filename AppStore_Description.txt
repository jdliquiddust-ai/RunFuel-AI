── DESCRIPTION ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

STRAVA TRACKS. RUNFUEL ARCHITECTS.

Strava tells you how far you ran. RunFuel tells you how to fuel it.

Most runners hit the wall not because they're unfit — but because they
guessed their nutrition. RunFuel AI ends that. It computes your complete
fueling plan from your actual body weight, distance, and race pace, then
guides you through every fuel stop with real-time countdowns.

─ THE CALCULATOR ─

Enter your weight, distance, and pace. RunFuel instantly computes:

  • Carbohydrates needed (grams)
  • Sodium target (milligrams)
  • Hydration requirement (fluid ounces)
  • Number of fuel stops and exact timing
  • Per-stop breakdown so you know exactly what to carry

Formulas are grounded in ACSM (American College of Sports Medicine)
endurance nutrition guidelines — the same standards used by elite coaches.

─ TRAINING MODE vs. RACE DAY MODE ─

TRAINING MODE  →  Standard fueling. ~45g carbs/hr. Build the habit.

RACE DAY MODE  →  Aggressive protocol. ~60g carbs/hr. Peak sodium.
                   Because Sunday is not a practice run.

Switch modes and watch the numbers recalculate instantly from your inputs —
no presets, no guesswork.

─ RACE INTELLIGENCE ─

Type your race name — "Boston Marathon," "Chicago Half," "local 10K" — and
RunFuel detects the distance automatically. Your fueling plan is then
computed specifically for that race distance, your pace, and your body.

Supports: 5K · 10K · 15K · Half Marathon · Marathon · 50K · 100K · Ultra

─ LIVE RUN ENGINE ─

When you're ready, tap Start Race. RunFuel switches to a live timer with:

  • A countdown ring to your next fuel stop
  • Fuel banners that fire exactly when you need to eat
  • Haptic alerts at every stop (Android)
  • A scrubable timeline so you can simulate or review your race
  • Elapsed time tracking

The screen stays on. The ring counts down. You run.

─ COACH'S INSIGHT ─

Below every fuel plan, a Coach's Insight card delivers a context-aware tip:

  "Half marathon distance. Your first window opens at 28 min — don't wait
   for hunger signals. Absorption takes 15–20 minutes."

  "20+ miles: glycogen hits critical at mile 16–18. Every scheduled hit
   is your wall insurance. Miss one and you pay for it later."

Tips adapt to your distance, pace, mode, and hydration demand.

─ FUEL BRAND PAIRING ─

Select your go-to brand and RunFuel tells you exactly how many to carry
per stop. Supports 14 brands including:

GU Energy · Maurten · Huma · Clif Shot · Honey Stinger · Spring Energy
SiS · Skratch Labs · Precision Fuel · Tailwind · Nuun · SaltStick
VESPA · SWORD Performance

─ RUN HISTORY & ACHIEVEMENTS ─

Every completed run is logged. Track your total miles, average pace, and
unlock achievement badges as you hit milestones: First Run, Habit (5 runs),
Half Century (50 miles), Marathon Month, Speed Demon, Long Hauler.

─ PRIVACY FIRST ─

Zero data collection. Everything lives on your device. No account required
to use the calculator. No ads. No tracking. No subscriptions forced on you.

─ BUILT FOR ATHLETES ─

  • Executive Noir dark interface — readable in bright sunlight
  • Designed for one-handed use mid-race
  • Works offline — no internet needed for fuel calculations
  • Instant calculations — no "calculate" button, results update live

WHETHER YOU'RE CHASING A PR OR FINISHING YOUR FIRST MARATHON —
you deserve to know your numbers before the gun goes off.

RunFuel AI. Architect your race.
