Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2026 · Last updated: May 2026

🔒 The short version: RunFuel AI collects zero personal data. Everything stays on your device. We don't track you, sell you, or store anything about you on our servers.

What We Collect

Nothing. RunFuel AI does not collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information. The app operates entirely on-device using your browser's local storage.

Information you enter — including your name, weight, run history, and fuel plans — is stored exclusively on your device and is never sent to any server, analytics platform, or third party.

Local Storage

RunFuel AI uses your device's local storage to save your preferences, run history, and profile between sessions. This data never leaves your device. Clearing your browser or app data permanently removes all stored information.

AI Coaching Tips

When you request a personalized coaching tip, anonymized, non-identifying run parameters (distance, pace category, and run phase) are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a response. No name, email, device ID, or location is included in this request. Anthropic's privacy policy governs that interaction: anthropic.com/privacy.

Health & Fitness Data

RunFuel AI does not request access to Apple HealthKit, Google Fit, or any health platform APIs. Any fitness metrics you enter manually are stored locally on your device only.

Analytics & Tracking

We do not use analytics tools, advertising SDKs, crash reporters, or tracking pixels of any kind. There is no advertising in RunFuel AI. There is no tracking. Period.

Children's Privacy

RunFuel AI does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is intended for adult athletes and endurance runners.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update this page with a new effective date. Given that we collect no data, material changes are unlikely.

Contact

Questions? Reach us at support@runfuelai.com.
