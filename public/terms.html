What RunFuel AI Does

RunFuel AI is an endurance nutrition planning tool. It calculates personalized fueling plans — carbohydrate intake, sodium, hydration, and fuel-stop timing — based on user-provided inputs including body weight, race distance, and pace.

Fueling formulas are grounded in guidelines published by the American College of Sports Medicine (ACSM), peer-reviewed sports nutrition research, and standard endurance coaching practice. Race Day protocol targets align with ACSM's recommendation of 60g carbohydrates per hour for high-intensity endurance events.

Scientific Basis

RunFuel AI's fueling math is derived from the following evidence-based guidelines:

  • Carbohydrate intake: 30–60g/hr (training), up to 60–90g/hr (competition) — ACSM/AND/DC Joint Position Statement on Nutrition and Athletic Performance.
  • Sodium replacement: 500–700mg/hr for events over 1 hour — ACSM Position Stand on Exercise and Fluid Replacement.
  • Fluid intake: 400–800ml/hr adjusted for body weight and intensity — ACSM guidelines.
